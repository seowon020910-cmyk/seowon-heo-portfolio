Overview

Assistant roles across multiple productions

This page documents my experience working as a costume, props, and stage assistant across several productions — supporting main designers, fabricating costumes, and operating technical elements.

Each project contributed to my understanding of how different departments collaborate toward a unified performance vision.

Song of Cheoyong · 2023

Costume Assistant

Role: Assistant Costume Designer (70%)
Period: May – Jul 2023
Venue: Red Gate Theatre, Seoul Institute of the Arts
Note: All costumes custom-made

Responsibilities: costume making, preparation for dress rehearsals, attendance at rehearsals, assisting during live performances. Pattern making for Cheoyong, the Plague Spirit, Gaya, Gaya's Mother, and the Fish character.

Production subsequently selected for La MaMa Spoletopen 2025 in Italy.

"All costumes were custom-made — pattern development, fabric selection, cutting and dyeing."

Assistant Stage Manager
