Background & Objective

Reinterpreting Korean culture through contemporary lens

This project explores the reinterpretation of Korea's distinctive cultural elements through a contemporary lens — combining traditional Korean music with modern dance to create a new form of artistic expression.

Team Talmude aims to express the beauty and uniqueness of Korean culture through music and movement, expanding its cultural value to a global audience. By blending traditional Korean music with electronic sound and translating these elements into contemporary choreography, the project invites audiences to experience Korean culture from a refreshed perspective.

The music video Soran (騷亂) seeks to reaffirm Korean cultural identity and contribute to its recognition on the global stage.

"Reaffirming Korean cultural identity through the fusion of traditional music, electronic sound, and contemporary choreography."
